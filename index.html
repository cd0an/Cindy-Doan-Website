Socket Programming Project

March 2025

This Python-based program simulates a simple client-server banking system. Once the server is running, multiple clients can connect locally by logging in with a username and password. After authentication, users can (1) make a transaction, (2) view transaction history, (3) check fees or account balance, or (4) exit the program. The system showcases how basic banking operations can be performed securely through client-server communication using sockets.

Accounting Business Proposal

April 2022

This project involved developing a full business proposal and preparing key financial statements, including the balance sheet, income statement, cash flow statement, and statement of shareholders’ equity. I created a Boba Tea business concept that not only focused on sound financial planning but also reflected a personal connection to my cultural identity. The project served as both an academic and personal statement during a time of heightened racial awareness amid the COVID-19 pandemic, highlighting how business can be used to empower underrepresented communities.
